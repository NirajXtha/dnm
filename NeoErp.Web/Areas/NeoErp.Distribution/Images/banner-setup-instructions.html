How It Works

  • Print Button: Click the "Print" button in the QR Generator to print unprinted QR codes
  • Banner Layout: Each QR code will be printed on a separate banner (800x400px)
  • QR Position: QR codes are positioned in the middle-right area of the banner
  • Auto-Print: After displaying the QRs, the print dialog will automatically open
  • Mark as Printed: Successfully printed QRs are automatically marked as printed in the database
